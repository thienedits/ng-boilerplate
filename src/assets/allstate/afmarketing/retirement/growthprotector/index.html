  • Spread the Word

    It’s clear that the retirement needs of today’s consumers are different than their parents. Many Americans are struggling to find ways to protect what they have today and are seeking options that will guarantee income and protect their current savings in retirement. Allstate's product suite meets those needs with the Allstate IncomeProtectorSM and Allstate GrowthProtectorSM.

    Allstate IncomeProtector

    Allstate IncomeProtectorSM is a deferred income fixed indexed annuity offering guarantees for retirement income — protecting what consumers have saved, while guaranteeing lifetime income for the future with flexibility to start taking retirement income when the time is right.

    Allstate GrowthProtector

    Allstate GrowthProtectorSM is a flexible premium fixed indexed annuity offering guarantees for retirement savings — helping consumers’ money grow, protecting what they have accumulated while simplifying their ability to save.
