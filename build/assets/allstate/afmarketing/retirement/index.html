Confidence in Retirement: Products

Retirement Products

Allstate Financial offers a selection of affordable financial products designed to help customers achieve the comfortable retirement that they deserve. Whatever a customer’s goals may be, Allstate can help put those goals within reach.

For a general overview of the annuity options Allstate offers, you may refer to our Annuities-at-a-Glance guide.

Annuities

Annuities offer customers a steady stream of income from their savings, generally with earnings being tax-free until withdrawn from the contract.

Expanded Markets

The Allstate Expanded Markets program provides additional core product offerings through strategic partnerships. This gives customers access to various products not available through Allstate Life Insurance Company, Lincoln Benefit Life, and Allstate Life Insurance Company of New York. As part of this program, we offer annuities sold by Prudential, Protective Life, and AXA Equitable.
