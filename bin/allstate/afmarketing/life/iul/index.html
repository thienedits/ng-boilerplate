  • About Ultra Index

    An indexed universal life insurance policy that provides:

    • Protection—permanent life insurance coverage with death benefit
    • Cash Accumulation—ability to build cash value (cap of 9%)—particularly through max funding
    • Downside Protection--and additional assets guaranteed with a zero floor.

    Is designed for customers who want death benefit protection but also want:

    • Upside potential
    • Downside protection
    • Flexibility
    • Cash Accumulation
    • Lower fees and loads
    • Less expensive coverage then GUL
