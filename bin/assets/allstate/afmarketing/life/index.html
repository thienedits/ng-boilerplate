Confidence in Life: Products

Life Insurance Products

Allstate Financial offers a broad range of affordable life insurance products designed to help protect customers and their families in times of loss. Whether term or permanent, Allstate life insurance can help families cover medical bills, funeral costs, mortgage or rent, college tuition and many other living expenses.

For a general overview of the life insurance protection Allstate offers, you may refer to our Life Insurance: Protect What Matters Most brochure.

Term Life Insurance

Term insurance provides customers dependable, low-cost protection with a specific period of coverage, affordable rates, and a guaranteed premium period.
