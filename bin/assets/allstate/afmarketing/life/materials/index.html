Declining UL Customer Notification Program

The goal of this program is to improve the customer experience by proactively communicating, beyond the annual statement, with specific Allstate universal life customers who are experiencing year-to-year declines in their life policy values and potential premature policy lapses. The Declining UL Customer Notification Program Guide helps you prepare for and understand how to assist customers with declining policy values. The program provides the opportunity to assist the customer in making the necessary changes to his/her policy to keep it from lapsing.
